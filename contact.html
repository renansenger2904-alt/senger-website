03Before you send

Common questions, answered.

The things people usually want to know before sending a brief.

How quickly will I get a response?

Within one business day, almost always within four hours during AWST business hours. A written fixed-scope proposal typically follows within 48 hours of our first call.

Can I send my drawings before we sign anything?

Yes. Files attached through this form are confidential and not shared. If you’d like a mutual NDA in place before sharing detailed documentation, tick the box on the form and we’ll send one through.

Do you do small jobs, or only large packages?

Both. A single FEA verification, a one-page detail, or a quick render quote are just as welcome as full multi-discipline mechanical packages. There’s no minimum engagement.

How is the work priced?

Fixed-scope fee for well-defined deliverables (most fabrication-drawing and render work), hourly for exploratory or product-development phases. The proposal lays out which model applies and why.

What format should I send my files in?

Anything you have. PDF, DWG, DXF, STEP, IGES, STL, SolidWorks parts/assemblies, photos, spec sheets, ZIPs. If it’s very large (over 25 MB), send a WeTransfer or Google Drive link in the proposal text.
