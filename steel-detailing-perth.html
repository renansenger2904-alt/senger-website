02How we work

Detailers who think like fabricators.

Bad detailing costs money on the workshop floor — missed cleats, wrong bolt sizes, weld symbols that don't match what the welder can actually run. We start every package by understanding the fabricator's setup: machinery, preferred weld types, standard connections, paint and finish processes.

Free 30-minute scoping call to walk through the engineering drawings, GA and structural design. Written, fixed-scope proposal returned within 48 hours, with turnaround quoted upfront for the whole package.

Typical projects span portal frames and industrial sheds, mining infrastructure, conveyor and chute structures, mezzanines and equipment platforms, and structural steel upgrades. Output includes 2D PDFs, native DWGs, NC files for the CNC, and revision-tracked drawing registers.

NDA available on request — your engineering drawings and our detailing models remain confidential.
