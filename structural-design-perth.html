02How we work

Calculations that survive review.

Structural calculations are only useful if they hold up under independent review and match what gets built. We design conservatively where the spec allows it, document the assumptions, and provide calculations a reviewing engineer can verify line-by-line — not a black-box export.

Free 30-minute scoping call to understand the project, site loads and any constraints (cyclonic zones, mine site categories, existing structures). Written, fixed-scope proposal returned within 48 hours.

Typical projects span industrial sheds and workshops, mining headframes and conveyors, steel mezzanines, equipment platforms, tank stands and bunding, and structural upgrades to existing plant. All output is to AS/NZS 1170 loads and AS 4100 for steelwork, with connection design and engineering sign-off included where applicable.

NDA available on request. Design files, calculations and reports remain confidential.
