Approach

Senger Engineering supports industrial, mining and architectural projects across Perth and Western Australia. The studio draws on direct, hands-on experience delivered through established Perth engineering firms — bringing the same rigour, documentation discipline and standards-driven workflow to independent engagements.

Every brief is reduced to first principles before geometry is committed. Loads are calculated, load paths are traced, materials are selected against the appropriate code, and the design is verified by hand or by FEA before drawings are issued. No assumptions, no shortcuts.

Australian Standards

Designed and documented to code.

Senger Engineering operates within the framework of Australian Standards. The references below describe the principal codes consulted across mechanical, structural and welding work — selected per project to suit the application and client requirements.

AS 4100
Steel structures

AS 3990
Mechanical equipment — Steelwork
Steelwork supporting mechanical and process equipment.
AS/NZS 1170

AS/NZS 1554

Engineering process

From brief to fabrication, in five stages.

  1. 01

    Brief & scope

    Engagement begins with a clear, written scope: deliverables, governing standards, client specifications, interfaces with other trades, and acceptance criteria. No work begins before the brief is agreed.

  2. 02

    Concept & first principles

    Loads are calculated, load paths traced and critical components sized by hand or spreadsheet before any 3D geometry is committed. The concept is validated against the brief and discussed with the client.

  3. 03

    Detailed design & analysis

    3D modelling and detailing in SolidWorks. Where required, FEA is performed for stress, deflection and fatigue verification, with mesh studies and convergence documented in a formal report.

  4. 04

    Documentation

    General arrangement, fabrication and shop drawings produced to construction-ready quality. Bills of materials, weld schedules, bolt callouts and surface treatment notes included as required for issue to fabricator.

  5. 05

    Manufacture support

    Drawing queries, fabrication RFIs and minor design adjustments resolved in collaboration with the fabricator and client. Revisions issued and documented through to as-built where required.
