01 · Mechanical & product engineering

A processing machine, from concept to fabrication-ready.

Sector — WA materials-handling / processing OEM

The challenge

An equipment manufacturer needed a processing machine taken from a rough concept to a build-ready package: structurally sound under operating and fault loads, manufacturable in a local workshop, and documented well enough that fabrication could start without a stream of questions.

Our approach

Loads, drive sizing and critical components were worked out from first principles by hand and spreadsheet before any geometry was committed. The machine was modelled in full 3D, then the frame and key members were verified by FEA under operating and fault load cases, checked against AS 3990 and AS 4100. A DFM/DFA pass with the fabricator in mind removed parts that were awkward or expensive to build.

The result

A verified design released as a single manufacture-ready package — frame, drive arrangement, guarding and bill of material — that went to the workshop and was built without a redesign loop. The hard questions were answered on the screen, not on the shop floor.

  • General arrangement & detail drawings to AS 1100
  • FEA verification report (von Mises, operating + fault cases)
  • Weld & bolt schedules, full bill of material
  • Exploded views and assembly guidance
02 · Structural steel & fabrication drawings

A steel portal frame, detailed and verified for the workshop.

Sector — Perth steel fabricator / builder

The challenge

A fabricator needed a steel portal-frame structure designed and detailed to code, with connection details and shop drawings clean enough to fabricate straight off the page — no interpretation, no improvising connections on the floor.

Our approach

Members and load paths were sized to AS 4100, and connections — bolt groups and welds — were designed to AS 4100 and AS/NZS 1554. The structure was modelled in 3D, then issued as fabrication-ready shop drawings to AS 1100, with weld callouts, bolt schedules, hole patterns and surface-treatment notes all on the page.

The result

Drawings the workshop built from directly, with every connection resolved before steel was cut. Fewer RFIs, no last-minute calls, and a fabrication pack the shop could release without chasing the engineer.

  • Design calculations & verification to AS 4100
  • Connection details (bolt groups, welds) to AS/NZS 1554
  • General arrangement & marking plans
  • Fabrication / shop drawings to AS 1100 with full BOM
