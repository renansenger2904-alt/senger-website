02How we work

Engineering that survives the workshop.

Most mechanical engineering proposals look great on a screen and fall apart on a fabrication floor. We design backwards from the workshop — what can actually be welded, bolted, transported and installed — then verify the maths once the design constraints are real.

Every project starts with a free scoping call: we read your brief, ask the questions a fabricator would ask, and return a written, fixed-scope proposal within 48 hours. From there you get a single engineer accountable to the project — no project-manager layer, no junior hand-offs.

Typical engagements range from a single FEA verification through to full multi-discipline mechanical packages for plant, equipment and structures. Industries we work across: mining and fixed plant, industrial fabrication, commercial buildings, materials handling, and custom equipment.

All deliverables are issued to AS 1100 drafting standard, with structural design to AS/NZS 1170 and AS 4100 where applicable. NDA on request — your drawings, models and IP stay confidential.
