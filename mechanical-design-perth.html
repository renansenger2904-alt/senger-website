01What's included

A complete design package, workshop-ready.

Every engagement covers the work from sketch to fabrication, no version-control surprises, no hand-offs.

  • 3D modelling in SolidWorks (and Inventor on request)
  • General arrangement (GA) drawings to AS 1100
  • Detailed component and assembly drawings
  • Tolerance stack-ups and DFM / DFA reviews
  • FEA analysis and design verification
  • Material specifications and Bill of Materials
  • Fabrication drawings with weld and finish callouts
  • Revision control and document register management
02How we work

Design that scales with the project.

Mechanical design at the scale of plant and equipment is where most engagements go wrong, too many revisions, too much rework, drawings that don't match the fabricator's machinery. We start every project by getting clear on what the fabricator can actually do, then design within those constraints from day one.

Free 30-minute scoping call to read your brief and ask the questions a fabricator would ask. Written, fixed-scope proposal returned within 48 hours. From there, single-engineer accountability through to handover, no hand-offs, no junior re-work.

Typical projects span custom equipment design, mining and fixed plant, materials handling, industrial machinery and commercial plant rooms. Output is fabrication-ready: GA drawings, detailed parts, BoM, weld maps and assembly sequences.

All drawings issued to AS 1100 drafting standard. NDA on request, your CAD models and IP stay confidential.
