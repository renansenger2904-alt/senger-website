03Standards & Compliance

Designed and documented to code.

Every job is designed and checked against the Australian Standards that apply to it. The main codes we work to are below.

AS 4100
Steel structures
AS 3990
Mechanical equipment steelwork
AS 4600
Cold-formed steel
AS/NZS 1170
Structural design actions
AS/NZS 1554
Steel welding
AS/NZS 5131
Fabrication compliance
AS 1657
Platforms & walkways
AS 1100
Technical drawing

Additional standards consulted as required, including NCC / BCA, AS 1428 access & mobility, AS 1418 cranes, AS 4024 machinery safety, and project-specific client specifications.
