02Capabilities

Six disciplines, one standard.

From the first line of geometry to the final drawing, everything gets the same level of rigour.

01

Mechanical systems & equipment design

Power transmission, kinematics, machine design, and rotating equipment, engineered for service life, not spec sheets.

  • Drivetrain design
  • Rotating equipment
  • Hydraulic systems
02

Steel frames & weldments for mechanical assemblies

Frames, skids, supports and weldments that carry mechanical scope, sized and detailed to AS 4100 / AS 3990 / AS/NZS 1554, ready for fabrication.

  • AS 3990 / AS 4100
  • Weldment design
  • Fabrication-ready
03

Modular & prefabricated systems

Prefabricated assemblies designed for transport, lift, and rapid site installation, minimising risk and accelerating delivery.

  • Transport optimised
  • Site-friendly
  • Modular logic
04

FEA verification & component integrity

Linear, non-linear, modal and fatigue analysis on mechanical components and weldments, assumptions interrogated, results correlated where test data is available.

  • FEA validation
  • Fatigue analysis
  • Code compliance
05

Fabrication & build-ready documentation

Drawings that build cleanly. We work weld-side with fabricators, tolerances, GD&T, and assembly sequences detailed.

  • Workshop drawings
  • BOM management
  • GD&T applied
06

Coordination with fabricators & contractors

RFI response, shop-floor support, and inspection sign-off, engineering doesn't end at handover.

  • RFI management
  • Inspection support
  • Site liaison
03Process

Brief & scoping

RFI response, weld-side queries, and inspection support through to commissioning.
