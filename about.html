About Senger Engineering

Engineering built on precision.

Senger Engineering delivers mechanical and structural engineering solutions designed for real-world performance, buildability, and reliable project execution.

Renan Senger
Director & Mechanical Engineer
Based in Perth and Director of Senger Engineering. Over 10 years of experience delivering engineering solutions across mining, construction, and architectural projects. Specialises in buildable design and fabrication-ready documentation.

Based in Perth, the practice supports the development of steel structures, machine systems, and custom mechanical equipment across Australia — bringing the full engineering lifecycle, from early-stage concept through detailed design and technical documentation.

Senger Engineering is equipped to handle complex problems and abstract briefs. Each project, structure, or system is treated as a unique challenge to be solved with sound technical judgement and practical efficiency.

Mechanical systems and equipment design

Structural steel design and connection detailing

Modular and prefabricated systems

FEA and structural integrity

Fabrication and build-ready documentation

Coordination with fabricators and contractors

Senger Engineering engages projects where coordination, technical accuracy, and constructability are critical, delivering complete engineering packages that move seamlessly from concept to completion.

Objective: Deliver engineering that works — in design, in fabrication, and on site.
01About the Team

Experienced. Multidisciplinary. Reliable.

Senger Engineering is supported by an international team of engineers and technical specialists, ensuring scalable delivery across project types and time-zones.

The team includes over 10 engineers and 3 dedicated modelling specialists, working across design, documentation, and visualisation.

All work is coordinated through structured workflows and consistent design systems, ensuring consistency, quality control, and alignment at every stage of the project.

This approach combines hands-on engineering expertise with scalable production capability, delivering both precision and pace.

02Our Engineering Approach

Engineering for real-world precision.

Senger Engineering operates from Perth, Western Australia, focused on clients who demand more than conventional engineering services. The practice combines a bridge to concept, validation, and practical execution — combining engineering experience, not just drawings.

Who we serve

Our partners include construction firms, manufacturer-builders, fabricators, and project managers across industrial, commercial, and infrastructure sectors.

How we think

We believe great engineering starts with understanding the real challenge. We question assumptions, validate decisions, and design with purpose.

Why delivery matters

Timely delivery is critical. We ensure buildability, procurement, fabrication, and construction logistics are fully connected from day one through completion.

Our engineering approach

We deliver high-end mechanical and structural engineering packages for fabrication and construction, with a focus on integrity, precision, and buildability.

How we work

We combine technical excellence with strong coordination. Our workflows ensure smooth execution, clear communication, and predictable results.

What sets us apart

We challenge conventional methods and focus on what matters — safe, buildable, and high-performing engineering that delivers on site.

We engineer solutions that are practical, buildable, and built to last. From concept to completion — engineered to perform.
